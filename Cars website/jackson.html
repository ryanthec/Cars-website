JACKSON STORM

About

Jackson Storm is a state-of-the-art, futuristic race car. His design incorporates advanced aerodynamics and the latest technology, making him a formidable competitor on the racing circuit

Storm is exceptionally fast and skilled on the track, utilizing his advanced features and training techniques to outpace older-generation race cars like Lightning McQueen.

His confidence may come off as arrogance, but it's a reflection of his unmatched skill set, making him the undisputed leader among the next-generation racers. Storm's ability to perform at the highest level, coupled with his swagger, establishes him as the epitome of a supremely confident and dominant competitor.
