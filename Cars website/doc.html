DOC HUDSON

About

Doc Hudson is a legendary race car from the 1950s, known by the moniker "The Fabulous Hudson Hornet." He was a three-time Piston Cup champion, establishing himself as one of the greatest race cars of his time.

Doc plays a crucial role in mentoring Lightning McQueen.
