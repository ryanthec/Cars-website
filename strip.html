STRIP WEATHERS

About

Strip Weathers, also known as "The King," has won the Piston Cup championship seven times, establishing himself as one of the most successful and respected race cars in the Cars universe.

Despite being a veteran in the racing world, Strip Weathers has maintained a remarkable level of competitiveness throughout his career, demonstrating both skill and endurance.

The King is highly respected by his fellow competitors and fans alike. His sportsmanship and professionalism on and off the track contribute to his positive reputation.
